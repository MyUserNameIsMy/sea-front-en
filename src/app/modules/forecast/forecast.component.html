Forecast of hydrodynamic processes in the Caspian Sea
Time:

This page shows forecast maps of wind wave and sea level parameters for the Caspian Sea region. The maps are based on calculations using the SWAN spectral wave model and the MIKE 21 flow model FM. Operational meteorological data provided by RSE "Kazhydromet" and ECMWF have been used for these calculations. The information is updated twice a week.

Unit of Hydrometeorological Research of the Caspian Sea of the Research Center RSE "Kazhydromet".
